About AUTHENFY

Deepseek is an advanced AI-driven platform designed to detect and authenticate videos, images, and audio recordings. Our technology analyzes every aspect of media to identify deepfakes and other manipulated content, ensuring that the information you trust is real.

Key Features

  • Advanced AI for video, image, and audio analysis
  • High accuracy in identifying deepfakes and tampered content
  • Fast detection and real-time results
  • Secure blockchain-backed authenticity verification
  • Easy-to-use interface for seamless experience

How It Works

Deepseek's detection process involves a series of sophisticated AI algorithms to analyze both the visual and audio components of the content. We break down the media into frames and chunks, use embeddings for face recognition and voice analysis, and run deep learning models like Xception to detect inconsistencies.
